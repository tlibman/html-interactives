Geometric Definition

A parabola is the set of all points P such that the distance from P to the focus equals the distance from P to the directrix.

d(P, focus) = d(P, directrix)

As you move the focus and directrix, notice how the parabola's shape changes. The vertex is always halfway between the focus and directrix!
